The musical fragments were taken from the following records produced by The Feher Jewish Music Center of Beth Hatefutsoth:

The Green Rooster - Nigun of the Rabbi of Kallo -
Traditional. Interpreters: Meir Rimon, Lev Kogan, Laszlo Rooth, Teddy Kling, Kenneth Krohn. NIGUNIM. Hassidic
Melodies A Beth Hatefutsoth record produced by Dr. Avner Bahat, director of the Feher Jewish Music Center.
BTR 9003 ACUM 1900

Shalom Aleichem -
Traditional. Modern arrangement by Lev Kogan and interpreted by Meir Rimon, Lev Kogan, Teddy Kling, Kenneth Krohn. NIGUNIM. Hassidic Melodies A Beth Hatefutsoth record produced by Dr. Avner Bahat, director of the Feher Jewish Music Center. BTR 9003 ACUM 1900

It Shall Come to Pass (Ve-ye'tayu)
Music: Louis Lewandowski, after Carl Frierich Zelter, soloist: Joseph Schmidt, organ: Paul Mania. Recorded on December 14th, 1929 in Berlin. THE MUSICAL TRADITION OF THE JEWISH CONGREGATION IN BERLIN. A Beth Hatefutsoth record produced by Dr. Avner Bahat, director of the Feher Jewish Music Center. BTR 9702 P. 1997

A Bessarabic Hassidic Song
Music: Joachim Stutschewsky. Interpreters: Emanuel Gruber - cello, and Michael Bogulavsky - piano. IN HASSIDIC MOOD. A Beth Hatefutsoth record produced by Dr. Avner Bahat, director of the Feher Jewish Music Center. BTR 9202 ACUM 1993

One Kid, One Kid (Had Gadia) -
Bucharest tradition. Choral works by Yehezkel Braun interpreted by the Israel Kibbutz Choir conducted by Avner Itai. YEHEZKEL BRAUN. Choral Works. A Beth Hatefutsoth record produced by Dr. Avner Bahat, director of the Feher Jewish Music Center. BTR 9602 ACUM 1996
