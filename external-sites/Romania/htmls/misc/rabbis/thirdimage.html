Rabbi Joel Teitelbaum of Satmar (Satu Mare), Transylvania, c. 1920
Rabbi Joel(ish) Teitelbaum was a rabbi in Satu Mare from 1928 until 1944. In 1947 he settled in Brooklyn, NY, and for some time he served as rabbi of the ultra-Orthodox Neturei Karta community in Jerusalem. Rabbi Teitelbaum was a prolific author whose writings contain long polemics against the Zionism and the State of Israel and one the other hand offered an explanation from an ultra-Orthodox point of view on the reasons of the Holocaust.
