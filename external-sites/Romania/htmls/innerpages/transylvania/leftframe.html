The Green Rooster - Nigun of the Rabbi of Kallo. Also known as The Rooster is Crowing (Szol a kakas mar - in Hungarian), this song is associated with Reib Eizik, the zaddik of Nagykallo, in Hungary. The melody, whose lyrics were interpreted as an expression of Jews' longing to the Land of Israel, was very popular with the hasidic Jews of Transylvania. This recording is a modern arrangement for four horns.
