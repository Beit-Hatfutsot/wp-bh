Oil painting based on Ber Frank Halevi’s
                             drawing (mid-19th century)

Rabbi Moses Schreiber, Chatam Sofer; was the rabbi of Pozsony (Pressburg, Bratislava) from 1806. His famous yeshiva attracted some one hundred of students. He was a decisive personality of his time with strong traditionalistic views. He fought against all kinds of changes and reformist movements.
