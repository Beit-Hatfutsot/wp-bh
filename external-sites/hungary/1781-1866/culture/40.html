Lithograph by Miklós Barabás (1810–1898)

Born as Mordechai Rosenthal, Márk Rózsavölgyi was a violinist and composer. For a long period he was a soloist of Gypsy bands; later he played the first violin in theatrical orchestras of Pest and provincial towns. He is the best-known Hungarian composer of “csárdás”, having composed some one hundred “csárdás”-songs.
