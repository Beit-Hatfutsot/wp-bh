Journalists and actors in the Artists’ Inn of Károly Hatvani (Schrank) (1858–1929), an actor, in Akácfa Street in Budapest. Among them: Jenő Heltai (1871–1957), Miklós Kúnvári, Imre Szirmai (1860–1945), Emília Márkus (1860–1949), József Keszler (1846–1927), Sándor Bródy (1863–1924), Ede Újházy (1844–1915), Emil Szomory (1874–1944), Endre Nagy (1877–1938), Oszkár Párdány, Béla Ágai (1870–1944).
