Sándor Hatvany Deutsch (1852–1913), modernizer of the Hungarian sugar industry, member of the Upper House of the Parliament, was raised to nobility in 1908. Lajos Hatvany (1880–1961) was a writer, literary historian, member of the Hungarian Academy of Sciences, and one of the founders and supporters of Nyugat (West, 1908–1941), the most important periodical of Hungarian literature in the 20th century; he was a patron of Hungarian literature. Ferenc Hatvany (1881–1958) was a painter and art collector.
