The oversized bimah, split by the arched bearing partition. The prayer hall dates from 1408. It received its present form in 1652 and was renovated several times, notably in the Baroque era. Fresh paint in a light beige color covers the walls. The polished parquet floor glistens with light from the two tall narrow windows. Not all electrified, the many hanging lamps recall a time when they were the only source of evening light.
