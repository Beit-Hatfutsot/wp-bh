North end of the inner face of the proscenium arch (away from the congregation). The lower border inscription reads, “Mi ya’aleh b’har Adonai, U’mi yakum bim’ko[m] kodsho” (“Who may ascend the mountain of the Lord” Who may stand in His holy place? Ps. 24:3). The restorer missed a final “mem” in the word “bim’kom.”
