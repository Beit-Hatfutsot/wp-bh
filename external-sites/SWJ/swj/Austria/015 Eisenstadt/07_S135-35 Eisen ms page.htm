Page from an illuminated 18th century Ashkenazic Seder Birkat Hamazon (After Meal Blessings) on display in the Austrian Jewish Museum in Eisenstadt. This page illustrates two of the many blessings of thanks to the Creator for His many acts of grace. “Blessed be Thou, our Lord, King of the universe, who executed the acts of creation,” and “Blessed be Thou…who makest different kinds of creatures.” (Original in: Royal Library, Copenhagen; Cod.Hebr. xxxii.)
