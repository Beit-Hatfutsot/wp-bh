Dr. Klaus Lohrmann, director, and Dr. Martha Keil, senior researcher at the Institute for the History of Austrian Jewry, discussing the work of the Institute with Rivka Dorfman. Martha Keil explained:

 

“We must educate the young children here in Lower Austria. They see few Jews in their life. They hear about Jews only from grandparents and have no concept of Jewish customs or a Jewish house of prayer. The older children are less interested in Jewish matters. Perhaps we did not educate them properly.”
