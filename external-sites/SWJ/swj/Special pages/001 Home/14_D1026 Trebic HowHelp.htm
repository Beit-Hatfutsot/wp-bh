The restored Jewish Inn, center and right, and the restored 17th century Neuschul Synagogue in Trebic, Moravia, in a photograph from our colleague, Arch. Ing. Jaroslav Klenovsky, Brno, consultant to the Jewish community in Brno on Jewish history and the Jewish built heritage in Moravia. His latest book is Jewish Monuments of Moravia and Silesia, published in Czech and English by Era in 2001.
