<p class=MsoNormal dir=LTR style='text-align:left;direction:ltr;unicode-bidi:
embed'>In Baja, Hungary, the synagogue building was remade into a municipal
library. Peter Wirth, the Budapest architect who designed this restoration and
that of the synagogue in Apostag, was careful to retain and restore the
now-empty Holy Ark. Note the columns and their gilded Corinthian capitals.</p>
