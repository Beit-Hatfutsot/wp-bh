Set in among the formerly Jewish houses on Subrtovo Square in Dobruska, Bohemia, is the neo-Gothic style mid-19th century synagogue building, now a church of the Czech Brothers. This photo, among others, was provided by our colleague, Jiri Fiedler, of Prague, author of Jewish Sights of Bohemia and Moravia. Fiedler, now at the Jewish Museum in Prague, is exploiting his extensive research in synagogue architecture toward composing an encyclopedia of the Jewish built heritage in the Czech Republic.
