Main synagogue portal, sealed with bricks. Paired stucco fire-pots “illuminate” the inscription: V’asu li mikdash v’shakhanti b’tokham (“And let them make Me a sanctuary that I may dwell among them”; Ex. 25.8). On the street side, another similar portal is inscribed, Ze hasha’ar l’Adonai, tzadikim yavo’u bo (“This is the gateway to the Lord, the righteous shall enter through it”; Ps. 118:20).
