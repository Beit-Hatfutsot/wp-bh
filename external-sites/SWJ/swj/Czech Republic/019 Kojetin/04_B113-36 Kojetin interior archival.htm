Interior view of the functioning synagogue—probably from the 1930s—showing Aron Hakodesh, seating arrangements and decorative lamps. Window grills to the women’s gallery were sealed off in the church renovation. The small circular window above the Ark, blocked off on the outside, is covered by the taller Ark introduced with the 1718 restorations. (Illustration after H. Gold, Die Juden und Judengemeinde in Mahrens.)
