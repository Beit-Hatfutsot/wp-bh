Rococo screen and tevah of the Scuola Italiana in their present location. Formerly mounted in front of the Heikhal before the synagogue was moved, the ornate screen is now positioned around the tevah. A red velvet huppah, formerly also over the Heikhal, now hangs above the tevah and graces the infrequent weddings. In contrast to the changes made in the Levantine synagogue, the bipolar arrangement of the Scuola Italiana was retained, keeping the benches parallel to the long walls.
