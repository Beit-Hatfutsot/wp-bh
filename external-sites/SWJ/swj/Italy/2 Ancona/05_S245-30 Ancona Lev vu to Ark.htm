The Levantine synagogue, Scuola Levantina, built in 1550, refurbished in Baroque style, and still in use by the Ancona Jewish community. The synagogue was installed here in 1879 after the original structure was demolished in 1860. The rich Baroque Heikhal under an elevated canopy dominates a high, bright room. Open balconies for the women were added in 1935.
