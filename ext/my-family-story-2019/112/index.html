הודיה יואב הכח שבך Hodaya Yoav The Power in You The story of Hodaya's grandmother Julie, who was born in Tunis, is of a woman who built her life with faith and prayer that enabled her to withstand life's hurdles. Her image at the center of the exhibit shows her praying to God, which is the most natural thing for her to be doing. She is portrayed as a type of upside-down tree, with the treetop below and the roots on top. She is a strong and steady tree trunk that connects to the sky with her roots. Her prayers and faith shine light on her life, including the most difficult times, as depicted by the rays of light in the exhibit. The letters of the word E-M-U-N-A-H (faith), attached to the milestones of her life, convey her lifelong faith. The letter H ( )ה that seals the word "Emunah" begins the word "Hemshechiyut" (continuity) as a symbol of the next generation's continuity, who continue her path.
