גבריאל סיף סיפור אגדה אמיתי When Gabriel interviewed his grandmother, one story she told kept returning to him: how his great-grandfather traveled from Russia (present day Poland) to South Africa. His grandmother then visited the hometown of her father 74 years after he left. Gabriel found that the story was structured impeccably, almost as if it was a fictional tale. It was a perfect story to turn into an animated video.
